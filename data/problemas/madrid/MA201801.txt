Dados $x \in \mathbb{R}$ y el determinante  
$$\Delta_n =
\begin{vmatrix}
    1 & -\frac{1}{2} & 0 & 0 & \cdots & 0 & 0 \\
    x & 1 & -\frac{1}{3} & 0 & \cdots & 0 & 0 \\
    x^2 & 0 & 1 & -\frac{1}{4} & \cdots & 0 & 0 \\
    x^3 & 0 & 0 & 1 & \cdots & 0 & 0 \\
    \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots & \vdots \\
    x^{n-2} & 0 & 0 & 0 & \cdots & 1 & -\frac{1}{n} \\
    x^{n-1} & 0 & 0 & 0 & \cdots & 0 & 1
\end{vmatrix}$$
calcule $\lim\limits_{n \to \infty} \Delta_n.$
